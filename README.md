# FORGE & GLOW — The Ultimate Performance & Wellness Protocol

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **FORGE & GLOW** is a science-backed, all-in-one system designed to optimize both your physical performance and personal grooming. It combines a rigorous Push–Pull–Legs (PPL) hypertrophy routine with a 30-day skin and hair transformation protocol.

---

## 🏋️ The Training: FORGE (PPL)
Engineered for longevity and maximum hypertrophy, the **FORGE** system is a high-frequency training protocol designed like a modern product.

- **Science-Based**: Research-backed exercise selections and rep ranges.
* **Interactive Guides**: Detailed step-by-step instructions, cues, and common mistake alerts for every movement.
* **Visual Frame**: Real-time reference for start, mid, and end positions.
* **Dynamic Navigation**: Quickly switch between Push, Pull, and Legs days.

---

## ✨ The Wellness: GLOW (30-Day Routine)
Balanced with the physical grind, the **GLOW** protocol is your daily wellness companion.

- **7-Day Rotation**: A cycling routine covering shampoo, oiling, and deep-care days.
* **Meticulous Timing**: Morning, afternoon, and night steps with precise time-blocks.
* **Wellness Tips**: Professional grooming advice (Detan, SPF, and Chemical Exfoliation).
* **Weekly Overview**: A bird's-eye view of your entire monthly transformation.
